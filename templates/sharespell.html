Share your poshmark closet automatically

ShareSpell is the simplest, most fun, and reliable way to share your closet on poshmark.

Features

  • Share your entire closet to boost sales
  • Adjust your sharing speed to avoid reCAPTCHA
  • reCAPTCHA audio alarm to let you know when sharing gets interrupted
  • Recover from internet issues so your closet order doesn't get messed up

Pause and Change Speeds

We share in reverse to maintain your closet order and we only share available items. You can also share other peoples closets!

You can pause the closet loading or sharing process at anytime and resume. We support two sharing speeds: standard and turbo. Turbo is more likely to trigger a reCAPTCHA and interrupt the sharing process, but don't worry - just click resume after solving the captcha we ShareSpell will continue where it left off.

Recover from internet connection issues

Lost internet during the sharing process? Just hit resume after your connection is backup and ShareSpell will pick up where it left off

Pricing

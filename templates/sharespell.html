FAQ

Is there a limit to how much I can share?

Nope!

Will this mess up the order of my closet?!

ShareSpell will always share your closet in reverse order so that the order is maintained. Just make sure that your browser window (your poshmark closet page where sharespell is running) stays open until the sharing is complete and you will not see changes in closet ordering.

How much should I be sharing?

You should share as much as you would do if you shared manually. Currently, ShareSpell will only share available items in your closet. If you have a large number of available items in your closet (say, 500-1000), we recommend keeping your total daily shares under 5000.

What if I lose internet connection while ShareSpell is running?

As long as your browser stays open, ShareSpell will remember where it left off and you can resume sharing once you're reconnected.
